About NIMELSSA SOUTHWEST

The NIMELSSA SOUTHWEST remains the formulation from various NIMELSSA CHAPTERS across southwestern part of the country that’s primarily aimed to constitute and coordinate the affairs of all chapters within the region to ensure there’s effective and active participation of all the chapters in any of the NIMELSSA NATIONAL and REGIONAL activities. NIMELSSA is all one, whether the NATIONAL NIMELSSA, the REGIONAL NIMELSSA, and CHAPTER NIMELSSA. Also, The NIMELSSA SOUTHWEST is structured to ensure to encourage unity and peaceful co-existence between the associating chapters.

Also, they’re pledge to bring about grassroots publicity to the mother chapters about any outstanding and professional schedule staged by the National body. NIMELSSA SOUTHWEST is aimed to Maintain a cordial relationship with Association Medical Laboratory Scientists of Nigeria (AMLSN) chapters in SOUTHWESTERN States. Promote Public awareness of Laboratory Medicine within members. Develop and Promote standard/ethics in the Medical Laboratory profession.
